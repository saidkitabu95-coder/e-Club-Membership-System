# Quick Start Guide - E-Club Membership System

## 🚀 Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Open the Application
1. Locate the `index.html` file in the project folder
2. Double-click it to open in your default browser
3. Alternatively, right-click and select "Open with" your preferred browser

### Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Click "Register" in the navigation bar or use the registration button
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name**: Enter your name
   - **Registration Number**: Enter your student ID
   - **Email**: Enter a valid email address
   - **Password**: Enter a password (min 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
   - **Department**: Optional
   - **Year of Study**: Select your year
3. Check "I agree to Terms of Service"
4. Click "Register"
5. You'll be redirected to login

### Step 3: Login
1. On the login page, enter your email and password
2. Optionally check "Remember me" to save your email
3. Click "Login"
4. You'll be redirected to your Dashboard

### Step 4: Explore Clubs
1. From your dashboard, click "Browse Clubs" or use the Clubs link in navigation
2. View all available clubs
3. Search for specific clubs using the search box
4. Filter by category
5. Click "View Details" to see more information

### Step 5: Apply for Clubs
1. From club details, click "Apply Now"
2. Or from dashboard, click "New Application"
3. Select a club from the dropdown
4. Write your motivation statement (why you want to join)
5. Click "Submit Application"

### Step 6: Track Your Status
1. Go to "Status" page from navigation or dashboard
2. See all your applications
3. View application status:
   - 🟡 Pending: Awaiting admin review
   - ✅ Approved: You're now a member
   - ❌ Rejected: You can reapply

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

- Click the moon/sun icon (🌙/☀️) in the top-right corner
- Your preference is saved automatically

### Search Clubs
- Use the search box on the Clubs page
- Search by club name, description, or category

## 🔐 Account Management

### Logout
- Click "Logout" in the top navigation
- You'll be redirected to the home page
- To access protected pages, you'll need to login again

### Remember Me
- On login page, check "Remember me"
- Your email will be pre-filled on next visit
- Still requires password to login

### Change Password
- Currently not implemented (frontend only)
- In production, add password change in profile settings

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### Data Not Saving
- **Solution**: Make sure localStorage is enabled in your browser
  - Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
  - Firefox: Options → Privacy → History
  
### Can't Login
- **Solution**: Check your email and password
- Try registering again with different credentials
- Clear browser cache and try again

### Page Not Loading
- **Solution**: Ensure all files are in the correct folder structure
- Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

### Applications Not Showing
- **Solution**: Ensure you're logged in
- Go to Dashboard first, then check Status
- Refresh the page to see latest data
